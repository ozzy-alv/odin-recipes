Lasagna Recipe


Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe, a great recipe for the whole family to enjoy! Good ole italian homemade style just like Nonna would make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 2lbs Ground bison beef
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tiny can of tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Chicken essence
  • Beef broth
  • Lasagna pasta
  • 2 tablespoons himalayan salt
  • mozzarella

Steps

  1. Pour about one tablespoon of olive oil into the pot and cook the ground beef inside the cooking pot till beef juice evaporates and give it a slight char
  2. Remove beef and start to add your seasoning while mixing in a little bit of tomato sauce
  3. Dice and add your onions into the cooking pot
  4. start adding the beef, if possible try to break the meat apart
  5. after pour the rest of the tomato sauce covering the beef
  6. season with salt to taste and stir
  7. In another pot start cooking your pasta, after 7 mins remove, strain and pour over cold water
  8. Leave the meat sauce slow cooking for about 45mins- 1hr for taste
  9. After done get your baking tray and start layering your meat lasagna
  10. One layer of meat sauce another layer of mozzarella and a drop of olive oil
  11. After finished cook inside the oven at 240 degrees fahrenheit untill the top layer of cheese melts and voila simple lasagna!
